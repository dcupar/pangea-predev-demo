About This Prototype

This prototype demonstrates the integration of pre.dev AI specifications into the Pangea platform. The mockups show both client-facing and agency-facing interfaces, showcasing how AI-generated technical specifications enhance the brief submission and opportunity evaluation process. All pages are static HTML/CSS for easy reference and Webflow implementation.
